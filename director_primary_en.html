Welcome remarks by the new principal of the new seed school

T. Phuong Sarath

18/October/2024 -
Primary Director of the New Seed School

Vision
According to the noble words of Teacher Phuong Sarath, the first director of the New Seed School, all students of the New Seed School are important cells for the national society so that they can participate in the development of Cambodia in the future. All teachers must train them not only in basic knowledge, skills and abilities, but also in educating them in good morals, ethics and good habits. He added that educating them in good morals, ethics and good habits is the most important factor, and focusing on quality as a priority is the guiding principle in carrying out various reforms.

About the teacher
 He said that in order to become a good teacher, teachers must change from traditional teaching methods to modern teaching methods to be more relevant to the actual context. He added that preparing teaching materials or lessons to be attractive so that students can easily understand, remember and remember is the key to making students not easily bored and bored with learning, in addition to using art in teaching with high quality and efficiency.

About Students
 He emphasized that in order for students to learn to know, learn to understand, and learn to be capable, all students must focus on three important points: understanding how to learn to know, understanding how to learn, and understanding how to learn to be capable.

Final Message
As the board of directors of the New Seed School, I have the utmost confidence that all students will become a strong driving force in building the motherland from generation to generation to be even greater through education. Thank you!

THE NEW SEED SCHOOL

The school is well aware that most students and guardians, especially students, often spend almost all their time on digital devices after free time from school or work. Spending time on these devices can have two main effects: positive and negative, depending on the individual. We know that the evolution of technology is becoming more advanced, and Cambodian citizens who are skilled in using technology are still limited.
*For the above reasons, the school has created this official website with the following objectives:
1. Orient students and guardians to understand how to use digital systems safely.
2. Promote the curriculum as well as various competitive activities that the school has launched and held.
3. Expand the understanding of the digital system to the guardians of all new schools and communities.
4. Adapt and adapt to the situation.
5. Train students to use digital systems for the right purpose.

THE NEW SEED SCHOOL

New Seed School would like to wish the public, guardians, and all philanthropists that recognizing that digital skills are in high demand both now and in the future, the school has also organized a computer literacy or digital skills training program for teachers, especially students from primary to high school in the direction of 1. 2. Train them to have digital leadership 3. Train them from ordinary students to digital students 4. Train their mindset to grasp the three levels of digital skills in the cause of keeping up with the industry 5.0 5. Train them to understand how to prevent themselves from various Internet scammers.
*Referring to the above reasons:
Please note that there is a shortage of funds on the school side as well as materials for the learning process to be taught, resulting in a bottleneck in this direction. As we appeal to the public, guardians, charities, and mercy to sponsor and sponsor our children, even if they are more or less, we are very happy and deeply grateful for the generosity of all and all of you in the mission of building human capital in the field of technology in the future in line with the motto "Digital Citizen, Digital Society".
